04 / METHOD & LIMITS

Evidence before
excitement.

01 Universe & exclusions

The model evaluates BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP, ADA, BNB, LINK, AVAX and LTC, then shows exactly five. All are established assets on major exchanges. Memecoins, stablecoins and thin listings are excluded. A $50M minimum Binance 24h quote volume is required.

02 Technical score

15m, 1h, 4h and 1d Binance candles feed EMA20/50 alignment, RSI(14), MACD(12,26,9), ATR(14), relative volume, recent support/resistance and breakout distance. More aligned timeframes score higher.

03 Derivatives & book

Binance USDⓈ-M funding and open interest are context inputs; top-20 spot-book bid/ask notional estimates immediate imbalance. Open-interest change and aggregate liquidations are explicitly shown as unavailable when no comparable keyless series exists.

04 Trade math

Targets and stops are volatility-aware and anchored to resistance/support. Displayed gain/loss includes an estimated 0.10% taker fee on entry and exit (0.20% round trip), but not slippage or tax. No order is placed by this site.
